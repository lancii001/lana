Horses

Different species

Andalusian HORSE The Andalusian excels in dressage and traditional Spanish equestrian pursuits, such as bull fighting and ranch work. The majority of Andalusians are gray; less common, although found, is bay, black, dun and palomino.

Arabian HORSE Arabians are famed for their stamina, and although they can be found in many disciplines, they rule the long-distance sport of endurance. Arabians can be found in the following colors: chestnuts, bay, gray, black and roan.

Mangalarga Marchador The horses are used for polo, jumping, endurance riding and leisure pursuits. Color: Mangalarga Marchadors are found in all solid colors with gray, chestnut and pinto most prominent
